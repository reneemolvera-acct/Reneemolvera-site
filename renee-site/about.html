About

A little about me

I am an educator, speaker, and accounting professional who is passionate about helping students and organizations transform data into meaningful business decisions. I integrate data analytics, artificial intelligence, and real-world problem solving into my teaching to prepare learners for today's rapidly evolving workplace. Through mentoring, innovative course design, and industry collaboration, I strive to equip the next generation of business leaders with the skills and confidence to thrive in an AI-enabled world.
